How It Works

Shop Online

Shop online from the local grocery store. We will try our best to get that product for you!

Order Online

Order Online by making secure credit cart payments through Paypal.

Relax

Groceries On Wheels does all the shopping for you. You will be notified with a text/call if any particular product is not available in store.

Delivery

Your order will be delivered right at your doorstep.

Our Charges


For Students

1. Students will be charged $8.99 for all the orders below $100.
2. For all the orders above $100 but below $200, we will charge them $4.99 per delivery
3. All the orders above $200 will be delivered free of cost.

All the other orders

1. All the orders below $100, will be charged $9.99 per delivery.
2. For all the orders above $100 but below $200, we will charge $6.99 per delivery
3. All the orders above $200 will be delivered at a cost of $1.99.
